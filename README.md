
# EduConnect

EduConnect is a collaborative platform designed to connect students and teachers, making learning interactive, engaging, and rewarding. With a clean interface and intuitive features, EduConnect helps classrooms thrive through knowledge sharing and healthy competition.

---

## Features

- **Student & Teacher Accounts:** Secure registration and login for both roles.
- **Ask & Answer:** Post questions by class, answer others, and build a shared knowledge base.
- **Like & Support:** Upvote helpful questions and answers to highlight valuable contributions.
- **Leaderboard:** Recognize top contributors and encourage participation.
- **Teacher Dashboard:** Manage, review, and highlight student questions efficiently.
- **Personal Profile:** Track your own questions, answers, and progress.
- **Privacy First:** All data is stored locally in your browser—no server required.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Clone or Download** this repository to your computer.
2. **Open `index.html`** in your preferred web browser.
3. **Sign Up** as a student or log in as a teacher:
   - **Teacher Login:**  
     - Username: `teacher`  
     - Password: `teacher123`
4. **Begin collaborating:** Ask questions, provide answers, and see your progress on the leaderboard.
